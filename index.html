About Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. Known for their speed, performance, and iconic design, Ferrari cars have a long and storied history in both road cars and motorsport.

Ferrari Models

Here are some iconic Ferrari models:

  • Ferrari 250 GTO - A classic model known for its beauty and rarity.
  • Ferrari Enzo - A modern supercar named after the company's founder.
  • Ferrari LaFerrari - A hybrid hypercar that showcases Ferrari's technological prowess.

Timeline of Ferrari Models

  • 1947 - Ferrari 125 S: Ferrari's first car, featuring a V12 engine.
  • 1957 - Ferrari 250 Testa Rossa: Iconic racing car known for its successes in endurance racing.
  • 1962 - Ferrari 250 GTO: Highly sought-after classic known for its racing pedigree.
  • 2002 - Ferrari Enzo: Named after Enzo Ferrari, a limited-edition supercar.
  • 2013 - Ferrari LaFerrari: Hybrid hypercar, combining electric motor with a V12 engine.
